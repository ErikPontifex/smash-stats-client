What Does This App Do?

This app presents data for Super Smash Bros. Melee singles players. All of the data is currently sourced from smash.gg, but data from other sources will eventually be added.

How To Use This App

  • To select a player, enter their tag into the search bar near the top of the screen and then click their name when it appears in the dropdown menu.
  • This app presents all sets for a given player as well as head-to-head information for that player versus the field.
  • Click on the 'Sets' bar to see all sets the player has logged on smash.gg
  • Click on the 'Head-To-Head' bar to see how the currently selected player fares against other players
  • Click on the rows of the head-to-head table to see the sets between those two players.
